Short description:
   - This is a simple game, multiplayer, auto-control
   - Player use a provided tool to create a brain
   - Brain is loaded into a tank, after that, tank is set in battle field
   - Game will simulate a battle, tank will move obey brain
   - Game over if there is only a tank in battle field
